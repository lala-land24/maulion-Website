Classic Filipino Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

8-10 skinless chicken thighs

1/2 cup of water or chicken stock

1/2 cup white vinegar

1/3 cup low sodium soy sauce

1/3 cup lightly packed brown sugar

Filipinos appreciate Adobo for its delicious and well-balanced combination of flavors, showcasing the unique blend of soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. Its simplicity in preparation, rich taste, and cultural significance make it a beloved and enduring favorite in Filipino cuisine.

Tinolang Manok (Chicken Tinola)

Ingredients

2 pcs chicken thighs (slice 1in-to 2)

3 slices ginger

400 ml water

1 tsp cooking salt

1 tsp fish sauce

Filipinos love Tinola Manok, a traditional chicken soup, for its comforting and light yet flavorful broth infused with ginger and green papaya. The dish's simplicity, coupled with its ability to bring warmth and nourishment, makes it a beloved and cherished part of Filipino culinary culture.

Filipino Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients

1/4 kilo tripe (or tuwalya, cut into squares, rinsed, scrubbed well with salt)

1 tablespoon Vinegar

1/4 Cup cooking oil

1 teaspoon atsuete seeds

1 medium red onion (peeled, chopped)

Filipinos love kare-kare for its rich and savory peanut sauce, complemented by tender oxtail and tripe, creating a unique and flavorful dish that has become a local culinary favorite. The combination of hearty ingredients and distinct taste makes it a beloved part of Filipino cuisine.
